Grocery Manager Help Guide

Buttons Overview

  • Add Item: Adds a new row to your list so you can enter Category, Product Name, Brand, Size/Details, Quantity, and Price (CAD).
  • Summary Report: Opens a modal to view saved summaries and create a new one after a trip (enter Date, Store, and Actual Cost; Estimated Cost is auto-calculated from picked items).
  • Statistics: Shows charts comparing Estimated vs Actual costs by date and store (based on your saved summaries).
  • Help: Displays this guide.
  • About Solution: Shows information about the app.
  • Save: Saves your current grocery list to browser storage (localStorage) on this device.
  • Download Data: Downloads your grocery list as a JSON file (named with your customer ID and date).
  • Download Summaries: Downloads your summaries as a JSON file.
  • Upload Data: Loads a previously downloaded grocery data JSON file into the app.
  • Upload Summaries: Loads a previously downloaded summaries JSON file into the app.
  • Logout: Signs you out and returns to the login screen.

Editing Your List

  • Type directly in the table to edit item fields.
  • Use the checkbox in the "Picked Up" column to mark items you‚Äôve bought.
  • Click the trash icon in the "Actions" column to delete an item.
  • Use the search box and the "Show only unpicked items" filter to find and focus on what you still need.

Backups and Transfers

  • Click Save to store your list locally in the browser (persists across reloads on the same device).
  • Use Download Data and Download Summaries to create JSON backups.
  • On a new device, use Upload Data and Upload Summaries to restore from those files.

Tip: Install the Google Drive app on your mobile device to easily export and import files between your phone and your laptop/desktop.

Using Google Drive (Mobile Tip)

To make moving files between your computer and phone easier, install the Google Drive app on your mobile device.

  1. On your computer, click Download Data and/or Download Summaries, then upload those JSON files to Google Drive (web).
  2. On your phone, open the Google Drive app, download the JSON files to your device.
  3. In Grocery Manager on your phone, tap Upload Data and Upload Summaries and select the downloaded files.

This flow simplifies downloading and uploading grocery data and summaries across devices.

Troubleshooting

Data not appearing after reload?

  • Ensure you clicked Save after editing.
  • Browser storage is device-specific and can be cleared; keep JSON backups.

Upload not working?

  • Verify the file is a valid JSON exported from this app.
  • If in doubt, re-download from the original device and try again.

Can't find downloaded files?

  • Check your browser‚Äôs Downloads folder.
  • Files are named like: grocery-data-CUSTOMER123-YYYY-MM-DD.json.
